About

Christians flock to commemorate the sacrifice of Christ with
a Passion play [senakulo] and 'libot' [procession]. Watch as
acting Roman soldiers with menacing masks and armors
[locals call them Hudyo] 'arrest' and 'crucify' Christ. Then
proceed to the St. Joseph Cathedral in time for the start of
the 'libot', featuring the icons of saints, Mother Mary and
Jesus mounted on carrozas [carriages], followed by devotees.
