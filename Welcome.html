To our Constituents, Friends, Researchers, Guests and Followers:

Welcome to Alaminos City – the home of the famous Hundred Islands National Park (HINP)!

Thank you for visiting Alaminos City Website.

We have been working hard to serve you best. We took great leaps to jumpstart progress in our aim for a greater and better Alaminos City. With this commitment to our people and guests, we also continue taking strides of making Alaminos City a hub and a haven of fun and excitement.

This Website offers you the best of Alaminos City – its great history, committed officials, friendly people, significant events and updates, public services, places, delicacies, products and a lot more.

On behalf of a very grateful city, may I take this opportunity to invite you to visit us here in Alaminos City and see for yourself the fruits of our commitment to serve you best.

We take pride in showcasing what our humble city can offer you, most especially our famous Hundred Islands National Park. We have 124 islands at low tide and 123 at high tide, fascinating caves and tunnels, new amenities and an array of island activities that would make your stay thrilling and enjoyable.

In your stopover, do not miss the chance to offer your petition at our 404-year old cathedral – the St. Joseph Cathedral Parish, dine in our Engineered-Kawayan Food Court, relax at People’s Park and taste our equally famous Alaminos Longganisa, “kakanin”, sea foods, and other mouth-watering delicacies.

Meet us, meet our friendly people – the Alaminians, we are happy you chose Alaminos City and Hundred Islands National Park.

Be a part of a great history, be a witness to the boundless beauty of HINP – be our GUEST!

MAYOR. BRYAN F. CELESTE

City Mayor
